I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in the Department of Computer Science at University College London, where I am a member of Software Systems Engineering group and CREST centre. Previously, I received a PhD degree from the National University of Singapore, and a Specialist Diploma from Saint Petersburg State University. My research interests include software engineering, programming languages and formal methods. The current focus of my research is on automated program repair (please see research and publications for more information).
